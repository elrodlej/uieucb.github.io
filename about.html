    How UIE Started

    In 2016, after dedicating years of work at IICC (Instituto de Investigaci√≥n de Ciencias del Comportamiento) Eric Roth Ph.D., decided to found a research center inside UCB mainly focused on tackling behavioral issues with a strong quantitative and experimental approach.
